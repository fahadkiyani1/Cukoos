The Crust House Story

Welcome to Crust House, Barakahu's premier destination for authentic, mouth-watering pizza and fast food. What started as a passion for creating the perfect dough has blossomed into a beloved local staple.

We believe that a great pizza starts from the bottom up. That's why our signature dough is prepared fresh daily, rested to perfection, and hand-tossed to order. Whether you are craving our decadent Cheese Stuffer, our unique Square Pizza, or our crispy Grill Burgers, every item on our menu is crafted with top-tier, locally sourced ingredients.

With hundreds of five-star reviews from our amazing community, our mission remains simple: Delite in Every Bite. We invite you to dine with us, grab a quick takeaway, or let us deliver hot, fresh happiness straight to your door.
